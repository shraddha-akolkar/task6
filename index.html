The foundation of Sahyadri Hospitals was laid in 1994 with the establishment of Pune Institute of Neurology (PIN)—a modest 16-bedded facility that evolved into Maharashtra’s first dedicated Neurology & Neurosurgery center. Over a decade of groundbreaking success paved the way for expansion, leading to the launch of Sahyadri Speciality Hospital in November 2004.

Today, Sahyadri Super Speciality Hospital, Deccan Gymkhana, stands as a premier multispeciality hospital in Deccan, offering a 225-bedded state-of-the-art facility. Combining medical expertise, advanced technology, and professional management, the hospital is committed to providing world-class healthcare solutions.

What sets Sahyadri Super Speciality Hospital, Deccan apart is its distinguished team of renowned medical consultants, many of whom are recognized for their expertise on national and international platforms. These specialists work exclusively within the Sahyadri Group of Hospitals, ensuring dedicated, focused, and unparalleled medical care across various disciplines.

The hospital’s excellence is further reinforced by a highly skilled and accountable paramedical and administrative team, whose unwavering dedication and commitment to quality consistently elevate patient care standards. Their continuous drive for improvement ensures that Sahyadri remains the best hospital in Deccan for those seeking comprehensive and specialized medical treatment.
